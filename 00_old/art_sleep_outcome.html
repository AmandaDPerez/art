Significant Main Effects:

  • Psychological Distress: Higher K6 predicts higher trouble sleeping.
  • Confirmation Concern: Higher confirmation concern predicts lower trouble sleeping.
  • Awareness/Expectation: Higher Awareness/Expectation predicts higher trouble sleeping.

Stereotype Awareness is not significant, but marginal.
